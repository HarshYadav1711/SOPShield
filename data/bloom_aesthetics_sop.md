# Bloom Aesthetics Clinic — Standard Operating Procedures

**Document version:** 1.0  
**Last updated:** Customer support reference

## Business overview

Bloom Aesthetics Clinic is a boutique medical aesthetics practice serving SMB clients in a calm, professional setting. Support agents must answer only from this document. If information is not listed here, do not guess—escalate to a human team member.

## Services offered

Bloom Aesthetics provides the following treatments (consultation required for first-time clients):

1. **Botox** — wrinkle reduction; typical session 15–20 minutes.
2. **Dermal fillers** — volume and contour; consultation required to select product.
3. **Chemical peels** — light and medium depth; not performed on active sunburn or pregnancy.
4. **Laser hair removal** — multiple sessions; patch test required for new clients.
5. **Microneedling** — skin texture and fine lines; 3–5 days mild redness expected.

We do **not** offer surgical procedures, dental work, or general medical primary care. Refer those requests to appropriate specialists.

## Booking and appointments

- Appointments are scheduled by **phone** (555-0142) or through the **online portal** at bloom-aesthetics.example/book.
- New clients need a **15-minute phone consultation** before their first treatment appointment.
- Please arrive **10 minutes early** with a valid ID and completed intake form.
- Same-day bookings are accepted only when availability is shown in the portal; call for urgent slots.

---

## Cancellation and rescheduling

- Cancellations or reschedules must be made **at least 24 hours** before the appointment time.
- Late cancellations (under 24 hours) may incur a **$50 fee** per our policy.
- No-shows may be charged the full treatment deposit where applicable.
- To cancel, call 555-0142 or use the portal link in your confirmation email.

---

## Pricing and payments

- Treatment pricing is discussed during consultation; we do not quote exact filler or laser package prices in chat without a chart review.
- We accept major credit cards, debit, and HSA/FSA where eligible.
- Payment plans are not offered; financing questions should be escalated.

---

## When to escalate to a human

Escalate immediately when:

- The client is **angry**, threatening, or uses abusive language.
- The client **requests a manager** or human agent.
- The question is **outside this SOP** (medical advice, legal, insurance disputes, custom discounts).
- There is a **complaint** about staff, billing, or clinical outcome.
- The same question was **not answered twice** after checking the SOP.
- **Low confidence**: the answer is not clearly supported by this document.

Escalation handoff: *"I'm connecting you with our front-desk team now. They'll follow up shortly at the number on your account."*

Support email for escalations: support@bloom-aesthetics.example (monitored business hours).
